Artificial Intelligence

The theory and development of computer systems able to perform tasks normally requiring human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and translation between languages. (Oxford Languages)

Artifical Intelligence (or AI) has accelerated development in many industries in the world today, and a lot of the things we can now do would be impossible without it. It is still a rapidly growing idea with the usage of self-driving cars expected to skyrocket over the next decade, but it does have its drawbacks and concerns have been raised by some experts.
